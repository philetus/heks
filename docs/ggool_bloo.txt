ggool
=====

* atomic document, digital equivalent of something between a page and a tweet
  - other atomic docs are deks (index of docs) and fanl (graphics/video)
  - collection of atomic docs is a hord

* implements slekt_ubl interface:
    . esy_reyt_ubl() -> (False, unless also reyt_ubl)

    . eksfand_ffokus()
    . raasy_ffokus()
    . lour_ffokus()
    . kantrakt_ffokus()
    
    . kleer_slekssn()
    . gotoo_preebbeeus_slekssn()
    . gotoo_nekst_slekssn()
    . tagl_slekssn()
    
    . doof()
    . nnakk(kut_feeld)

* implements reyt_ubl interface:
    . esy_reyt_ubl() -> True
        
    . enet_nod(kee)
    . ensrt_glef(kee)

    . kut()
    . faast(kut_feeld)


* ggool nodes:
  - initiated by <heks><[glef]> sequence
  - <l>eyn: as in poetic verse, or a sentence
  - <r>aa: a subphrase, an item in a list, constituent element of leyns and spels
  - <f>ala: a word (palabra [latin parable]), composed of leaf nodes
          > (gram)
          > <tood>_(ss) (rough size)
          > [dasyo] id in series of given size (ex ip address)
          > [dasyo]_(tt): ordering - [a]_(tt) -> 0th, 'awtth', first
                                     [y]_(tt) -> 2th, 'yayth', third
          > {nnot}: 
  - <k>want: numeric quantity composed of tood and dasyo (opt unit and error)
           (raa of kwants -> vector)
  
  leaf nodes:
  - <g>ram: phonetic atom associated with a meaning
  - <t>ood: numeric magnitude
  - <d>asyo: numeric fraction (pedazo - piece)
  - <n>not: emote/mode/emphasis
            {d} -> !
            {l} -> ?

            {u} -> :)
            {o} -> :(
            {s} -> :P
            {g} -> ;)
            {b} -> :/
            {f} -> <3
            
            {k} -> marked as unique
            {a} -> link to mark
            {h} -> tagged as kind
            
  
* key input (heks board has 16 glefs, <heks> and <trgr> keys
  - can be implemented with nintendo controller (4-way D-pad, A & B buttons)
      . D-pad alone:             [^]: a -     [<]: e -     [>]: u -     [v]: o  
      . A-button + D-pad:      [A+^]: k -   [A+<]: t -   [A+>]: b -   [A+v]: d
      . B-button + D-pad:      [B+^]: y -   [B+<]: s -   [B+>]: h -   [B+v]: f
      . A+B-buttons + D-pad: [A+B+^]: l - [A+B+<]: r - [A+B+>]: n - [A+B+v]: g
      . A-button alone: <trgr>
      . B-button alone: <heks>
  - <[glef]>: if in leaf node, attempt to insert glef, otherwise attempt to init
              appropriate leaf node for context and then insert glef  
  - <heks><a|e|u|o> navigation:
      <heks><a> - expand focus
      <heks><e> - move to earlier node
      <heks><u> - mode to later node
      <heks><o> - contract focus
  - single <heks> followed by <[glef] except y/vowel>: attempt to initiate node
  - <trgr>: moves forward to next item in context
  - <heks><heks>: undo
  - <heks><trgr>: redo
  - <heks><y>: break out of current (ggool/deks/fanl) context
  - <heks><s>: slekt mode (can be broken out of with <heks><y>)
    . node inits <trgr> and <heks><[l|h|b|r|f|k|g|t|d|n]> are remapped
    . <trgr> toggles selection
    . <heks><s> clears slekssun
    . <heks><k> kut_s slekssun (to faast buffr) (non-contiguous slekssuns added
                as separate fields)
    . <heks><d> doop_s slekssun to faast buffr (like kut but non-destructive)
    . <heks><f> faast_s from top of buffr, replacing current selekssun
                (each discrete non-contiguous selekssun is replaced)
    . <heks><n> nnakk_s top of faast buffr and adds matches to selection
    . <heks><b> opens faast buffr (<heks><y> breaks out of it)
                 # <heks><a> creates new blank field at top
                 # <heks><o> deletes current field
                 # <heks><e> and <heks><u> rotate buffr
                 # <trgr> opens current field for editing (<heks><s> deletes
                   at focus, <heks><y> breaks all the way out of buffr)
